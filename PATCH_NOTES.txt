## v0.1.0.2 - Raffle Entry Delete Fix

- Fixed row-level Delete in the Statistics tab so the confirmation popup opens reliably.
- Improved the delete confirmation text to show the selected player, ticket range, and cost before rebuilding later ticket ranges.

- Split the Statistics tab row actions into separate Tell and Delete columns so both buttons remain readable.
